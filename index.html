A closed workshop event that brings together fundamental research talks and discussions within the intersection of distributed systems and data analytics.

Day 1 will be mainly dedicated to talks around algorithms and mechanisms for decentralized data management and analytics. Day 2 will focus on system support for scalable, continuous and fast data analysis.

Note: The workshop is collocated with the public PhD defence of Paris Carbone, which will take place at KTH Electrum the 28th of September.
